Admission going on for Fall Semester 2026

Presidency University is pleased to announce that the admission process for the Fall Semester 2026 is now open. We invite applications from talented and ambitious students to join our diverse academic community.

Detailed information regarding eligibility criteria, application procedures, and important dates can be found on our official admission portal.

How to Apply:

  • Visit the online admission portal at apply.presidency.edu.bd
  • Complete the application form with all necessary details
  • Upload required documents (Academic transcripts, photos, etc.)
  • Pay the application fee via online payment gateways

For any queries related to admission, please contact our admission office at +880 1234-56789 or email us at admission@presidency.edu.bd.
